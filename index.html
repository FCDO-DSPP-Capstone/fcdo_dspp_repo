1. United Nations votes in the General Assembly

This grpahs is made using all the votes in the general assembly from 1990 to 2024. The votes are condensed into a 2 dimensional space for visualization purposes. Countries that are closer together are countries that vote together more consistently than countries that are far apart

2. United Nations General Opening Speeches

Analysis of the opening speeches give by each country in years 1994 to 2023. Each country gives one speech per year. Each speech is split into many sentences. Then, each sentence is classified into a topi. Then, we selected only the technological related topics and make this plots.

3. Network of Technology Research

Country Collaboration Network. Countries are counted based on the country of affiliation of the institution to which a researcher is ascribed.

4. Network of Global Trade

Trade between countries using selected products related to semi conductors and chips.
